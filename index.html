Team

Van der Stocken Tom

Professor of Ecology

Tom Van der Stocken is a professor of Ecology at VUB since 2022 and a research affiliate at NASA-Caltech JPL since 2014. His research focuses on tropical wetlands, global change biology, and coastal and ocean studies, using methods like modeling and remote sensing. He collaborates with international partners such as NASA, UCLA, and Moss Landing Marine Laboratories. Tom was a Marie Skłodowska-Curie fellow (2020-2022) and a postdoc at Caltech and NASA JPL (2017-2019). He earned his PhD in 2015 on mangrove ecology and received the Ignace Vanderschueren PhD Prize in 2020.
